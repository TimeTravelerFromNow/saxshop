Welcome to SteveGraySaxes.com

I buy, sell, repair and trade band instruments of all kinds.

I specialize in the Woodwinds. Vintage, new and used flutes, clarinets, saxophones and the double reeds.

I also have Brass Winds such as trumpets, coronets, trombones, etc.

Guitar and guitar amplifiers are also a specialty of mine, concentrating on Gibson, Fender and Martin guitars.

Last but not least are the Orchestral Strings such as the violin, cello and basses.

Also see the fine line of Miscellaneous instruments.
